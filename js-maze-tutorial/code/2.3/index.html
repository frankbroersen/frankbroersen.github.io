Changing the chance of x vs y movement

Please note, this is a % of chance, so there is still a chance that the mazes are rendered exactly the same way.

66% y / 33% x

This maze has a small chance to move to the right side of the maze

50% y / 50%x

This maze has a bigger chance of exploring the right side of the maze

25% y / 75% x

This maze has a very big chance of moving right on the maze, you might see horizontal lines

75% y / 25%x

This maze has a very small chance of moving right on the maze, you might see vertical lines
